# EVAチェック（施術者用） / EVA Clinician Checklist

## Evidence

- [ ] 疾患/症状に関する標準的治療・受療の選択肢を簡潔に説明した  
      Briefly explained standard treatment and care options for the condition/symptoms.

- [ ] 鍼灸（/CAM）の期待できる点と不確実性を説明した  
      Explained what can be expected from acupuncture (/CAM) and its uncertainties.

- [ ] 緊急/要受診のレッドフラッグを簡潔に確認した  
      Briefly checked for red-flag symptoms that require urgent or medical attention.

## Values

- [ ] 患者の希望・価値観・優先順位を聞いた  
      Asked about the patient's wishes, values, and priorities.

- [ ] 患者が許容できる負担（費用/時間/痛み/通院頻度）を確認した  
      Confirmed the level of burden the patient can accept (cost, time, pain, visit frequency).

## Accountability

- [ ] 限界と代替案（必要なら医療連携）を説明した  
      Explained limitations and alternative options (including medical referral if needed).

- [ ] 合意した目標と見直し時期を明確にした  
      Clarified the agreed goals and the timeframe for review.

- [ ] 記録に一文の要約を残した  
      Left a one-sentence summary in the clinical record.

---

## 記録用1文テンプレ / One-sentence record template

**Japanese**  
Evidence/Values/Accountability を確認し、患者の希望に沿って本日の方針に合意した。必要時は医療機関受診を含め再評価する。

**English**  
Confirmed Evidence / Values / Accountability and agreed today’s plan in line with the patient’s wishes.  
Will reassess, including referral to a medical institution when necessary.
